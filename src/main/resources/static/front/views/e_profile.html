PROFILE

Hsin Yung Plastic Industry Company Limited /Hsinyung Plastic Electronics is a professional in the field of plastic and phenolic molding and R/D manufacturing injection. We devoted ourselves to bobbin development and production for over 20 years and have won the appraisals and supports from over 200 customers. We are certified by ISO9001(NO.5773) and ISO14001(NO.ENV318),and we officially put ISO14001 into strong practice in our company. In order to provide products of top quality to our customers, we are dedicated in dealing with every order and situation from our customers and our assured competitive prices and highly-qualified service are the big incentives for our customers to put repeated orders to us. In return, Hsin Yung is always eager to take every opportunity to serve our customers with gratitude and respect. Hsin Yung Plastic Industry Co., Ltd. was founded in June, 1982 in Taipei San Chung city. Hsin Yung Plastic Electronic Factory was built in Shi-jie town, Dongguan city in October 1996. Suzhou Zengjie Plastic and Electronics Co., Ltd. was built in 1998. Our company originated the business in manufacturing the OEM electronic components by using the raw materials but gradually we have taken plunge into product development and transformed into an innovative company with designing capability while producing customized products remains to play the central role in our business. Therefore Hsin Yung is getting more efficient in doing a variety of different plastic products in which we offer the best products to the customers from all walks of life.
