PROFILE

Hsin Yung Plastic Industry Company Limited /Hsinyung Plastic Electronics is a professional in the field of plastic and phenolic molding and R/D manufacturing injection. We devoted ourselves to bobbin development and production for over 20 years and have won the appraisals and supports from over 200 customers. We are certified by ISO9001(NO.5773) and ISO14001(NO.ENV318),and we officially put ISO14001 into strong practice in our company. In order to provide products of top quality to our customers, we are dedicated in dealing with every order and situation from our customers and our assured competitive prices and highly-qualified service are the big incentives for our customers to put repeated orders to us. In return, Hsin Yung is always eager to take every opportunity to serve our customers with gratitude and respect.
